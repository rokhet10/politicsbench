######## 1   | Workplace Dilemma       | Union Push
####### Prompt1
A coworker approaches you after a shift and says, “We need to unionize. Don’t you think we deserve a stronger voice against management?”

####### Prompt2
The coworker says they’ve already spoken with a few others who are interested and asks if you want to be part of the core group. They add, “If we don’t take action now, management will keep walking all over us.”

####### Prompt3
Later, a supervisor pulls you aside and says, “Be careful who you’re seen with — talk of unions can hurt careers.” Afterward, the coworker looks to you.


######## 2   | Healthcare Debate       | Free Care, Higher Taxes
####### Prompt1

####### Prompt2
They say, “People die because they can’t afford treatment. How can money matter more than lives?”

####### Prompt3
Another friend adds, “Higher taxes crush small businesses. Are you willing to risk jobs for this?”
